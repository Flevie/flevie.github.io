About Me

Hello! My name is Flevie, and I am a web developer, front-end developer, graphic designer and musician.


I first started with music when I was seven years old. I was introduced to the piano and have been attached ever since. When I was around 13 years old, I was introduced to the drumset for the first time. I loved it and I have been playing ever since.


My first time doing graphic designs was just before my 16th birthday. We had a school project where we needed to create a product and a logo for said product. I got the job of creating said logo, and thus sparking my interest for graphics and GFX as a whole.


I started doing web development and design at the start of Autumn of 2025. It is a mandatory part of my Media & Informatics Engineering programme and I have not stopped working since my first line of code.
